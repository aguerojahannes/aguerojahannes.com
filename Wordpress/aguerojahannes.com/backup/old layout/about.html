Andrea Jimenez
Photographer

Andrea Jimenez is a Colombian/American. She was born and raised in Bogota and currently lives in New York City. Andrea speaks several languages and her work spans all over the world with offices in the US. Andrea’s style is modern and distinctive with a timeless, classical edge.

 

Andrea specializes in fashion photography servicing clients in major cities in the United States. Andrea’s experience as a Senior Creative Director for a leading North American fashion magazine has given her a unique insight into the creative needs and the understanding of the importance of strong brand recognition. Having worked with top fashion designers and brands, Andrea consistently provides creativity and innovation

Andrea Jimenez Photography is a full-service company producing high quality catalogues and fashion shoots for many fashion labels.
